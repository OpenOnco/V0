# Preoperative ctDNA and tumor volume predict colorectal cancer recurrence after metastasis resection.

## Abstract

GALAXY sub-study analyzing 181 patients with CRC liver metastases and 48 with lung metastases. Developed predictive model integrating preoperative ctDNA levels with radiographic tumor volume. Among liver metastases patients, median PFS was 11.4 vs 24 months in high vs low risk groups by ctDNA model. Among lung metastases patients, median PFS was 12 months vs not reached.
