# A plasma-based DNA test for quantification of disease burden in AML patients undergoing bone marrow transplantation.

## Abstract

Personalized v96 assay (up to 96 mutations) in 30 AML patients. 100% had detectable residual leukemia vs 20% by conventional assays. cfDNA from plasma more sensitive than bone marrow DNA. 352-fold higher mutant burden in relapsed vs non-relapsed patients.
