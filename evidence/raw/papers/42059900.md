# Early ctDNA stratifies survival in locally advanced and oligometastatic lung cancer treated with radiotherapy.

## Abstract

Phase II trial (n=26) plus validation cohorts (n=403). ctFE burden strongly stratified OS and PFS. Baseline ctFE prognostic for OS (HR 5.93) and PFS (HR 11.08). Three molecular response groups with OS separation (60.8 vs 13.0 vs 2.9 months). Uses Tempus tumor-naive ctDNA assay.
