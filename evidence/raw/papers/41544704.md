# ctDNA-guided immunotherapy following radical cystectomy for muscle-invasive bladder cancer: results from the TOMBOLA trial.

## Abstract

TOMBOLA is a Danish, multicenter, open-label, single-arm phase II trial evaluating serial circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) testing to guide postoperative immunotherapy in muscle-invasive bladder cancer (MIBC). 192 patients with MIBC (cT2-4aN0-1M0) treated with neoadjuvant chemotherapy and radical cystectomy were monitored with tumor-informed ctDNA assays. ctDNA-positive patients initiated atezolizumab for up to 1 year. Among 178 assessable patients (median follow-up 34 months), 104 (58%) were ctDNA-positive within 2 years after RC. Median lead time from ctDNA detection to imaging-confirmed recurrence was 90 days. 60% (50/84) of ctDNA-positive patients achieved complete response. 1-year RFS was 97% in ctDNA-negative vs 76% in ctDNA-positive patients.
