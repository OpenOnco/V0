Quick results Did you mean Medical Policy: L-267-001 Topic: NavDx Section: Laboratory Effective Date: January 29, 2024 Issued Date: January 29, 2024 Last Revision Date: December 2023 Annual Review: December 2023 Head and neck cancers may be caused by human papillomavirus (HPV). NavDx is a clinically validated circulating tumor-tissue–modified HPV (TTMV®) DNA blood test that aids in the detection of HPV-driven cancer. TTMV HPV DNA identifies a biomarker from cancer that has shed into the blood. Policy Position Serum biomarker testing (e.g., NavDX) may be considered medically necessary when ONE or more of the following criteria have been met: Individual is prior to surgery for HPV-driven head and neck cancer; or Individual is within 5 years post-operative of having HPV-driven head and neck cancer. Serum biomarker testing not meeting the criteria as indicated in this policy is considered not medically necessary.
